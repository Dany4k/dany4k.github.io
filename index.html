01.About Me

Hello! My name is Daniel and I am a software engineer with a big focus on Front End Development.

I have over 5 years of front-end development working with HTML, CSS and Javascript. This includes frameworks like Wordpress, Drupal, Prestashop, Blazor, Microsoft Power Pages and Angular. I have also done some small projects in Android Studio, MS Power Automate and MS Power BI.

During these years I have worked for smaller startups, state owned companies like IP (Infraestruturas de Portugal) and big consultant companies like PwC (PricewaterhouseCoopers) building websites, portals and various web applications.

My most recent work experience was as a Front-End Developer and WordPress Specialist at SPMS (Serviços Partilhados do Ministério da Saúde). I worked with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and the WordPress framework. My daily activities included adding new features to websites, fixing bugs, providing customer support to all SNS entities and conducting training sessions for these entities.
